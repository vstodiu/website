The studio

Small team, full arcade.

VSTODIU started as a way to keep shipping things that were fun to make, not just fun to pitch. No investors to satisfy, no roadmap decided in a boardroom — just games built because they needed to exist.

We move between platforms depending on what the idea needs: a roguelite belongs on a keyboard, a painting sandbox belongs in a headset, a strategy game belongs in a browser tab open next to twenty others. The medium follows the idea, not the other way around.

We're small on purpose. It keeps every decision close to the work.
